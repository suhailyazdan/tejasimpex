Lilliput Hot Beverage Machine

We are one of the leading manufacturers and suppliers of Lilliput Hot Beverage Machine in India. This machine is developed on the concept of in cup dispensing. It is a 2 stage dispensing machine well manufactured with quality features. The working of this Lilliput Hot Beverage Machine is easy and completely efficient.

At first the premix needs to be added in the cup. The water is heated in a pressurized tank ensuring the best end cup temperature consistently of 90 deg. The dispensing rate of ourLilliput Hot Beverage Machine ranges up to 5 cups per minute.

Our Lilliput Hot Beverage Machine are available in various options:

  • Sachet.
  • Pre-packed cups.
  • Canister in machine.

The Lilliput Hot Beverage Machine offered by us is among the cheapest available in the market. Extremely easy and low cost of operations this machine is highly advantageous since the maintenance is based on ensuring the best ROI. This Lilliput Hot Beverage Machine can be availed in different grades as per the specifications of our clients in market leading prices.
